Effective date: April 8, 2026

Tactics Journal ("we", "us", "our") operates tacticsjournal.com. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

- Email address (for account sign-in, trial/pro access, and newsletters)
- Account settings (theme, font size, email preferences)
- Billing/access webhook events related to subscriptions and purchases

How we use your information

- Send magic-link sign-in emails
- Provide account access and paid/trial/free content gating
- Manage newsletter preferences and audience tags
- Process and reconcile billing state changes

Services we use

- Resend (transactional magic-link emails)
- Buttondown (newsletter delivery and tags)
- Gumroad (billing and subscription events)
- Cloudflare Pages/Workers + D1 (hosting, functions, and data storage)

Cookies

We use an HttpOnly session cookie to keep you signed in to your account. This cookie is required for authenticated features.

Data retention

- Account records are stored while your account is active
- Billing/webhook logs are retained for reconciliation and troubleshooting
- You can request account deletion via the contact page

Your rights

You can request correction or deletion of your data by contacting us at:
https://tacticsjournal.com/contact/

Changes to this policy

We may update this page from time to time. Updates are posted here with a revised effective date.
